Program

APIs are disrupting industries from the inside and from the outisde. These software interfaces enable Enterprises and Startups to be more agile and more open to create multiple kind of business or innovative ecosystems.
APIdays London next 23-24th of September will focus on APIs in the Banking and Fintech industries for delivering state of the art business cases and technical best practices to upgrade models and legacy software with APIs, with worlwide experts from the banking and fintech industry that are actually working on this topic.
